About Me

Welcome! I design learning experiences that are clear, engaging, and focused on real outcomes. Outside of work, I’m a pottery instructor at a local nonprofit, making ceramics approachable for people of all ages.

I like learning by experimenting—testing new technologies, playing with GitHub, xAPI, and AR apps, and seeing what’s possible. I’m a big fan of DevLearn, free online technical training, and building things that are both creative and aesthetic—whether that’s eLearning or art.

When I’m not teaching or tinkering with tech, you’ll probably find me covered in clay, sipping coffee ☕, or thinking about my next side project.
